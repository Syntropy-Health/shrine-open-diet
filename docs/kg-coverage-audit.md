## Implications for Paper 1 HDI Recall metric

HDI Recall measures how often the panel surfaces gold HDI claims for scenarios drawn from the HDI-Safe-50 panel. The metric's denominator is in-panel — it does NOT measure recall against the broader HDI universe.

At 86.2% coverage of the curated public-reference universe, this is an **in-scope HDI recall** metric. The paper Limitations section must say so explicitly: "HDI Recall is computed against the HDI-Safe-50 reference panel, which covers approximately 86% of the curated public HDI universe known to NIH ODS and NCCIH (per `docs/kg-coverage-audit.md`)."

## Update procedure

1. Pull recent NIH ODS herb factsheets and NCCIH herb-drug summaries.
2. Append new (drug, herb, severity, source) entries to `research-journal/shared/datasets/hdi-public-references.json`.
3. Re-run `python3 -m scripts.kg_coverage_audit` to refresh this doc.
